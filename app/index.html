Climate Conference

CC22 Summit is a climate conference dedicated to teaching others about the devastating effects of climate change and what we can do to combat it. Our mission is to ensure that our planet will be habitable for our future generations. All the events at our conference align with this goal and we hope that you walk away from your computer with the initiative to save our planet. So join us for this four-day conference, where each day is a different theme that'll teach you about different aspects of global warming.

Reforestation

The theme for Day 1 in our 4-day event is reforestation. All our activities and events will revolve around this very important topic. We welcome you to a great day filled with guest speakers, virtual tours, and more!

More

Physical Pollution

Day 2 in our 4-day virtual event has the theme of physical pollution. Explore this day via our talented guest speakers, fun activities such as our recycling competition, and a whole lot more!

More

Transportation

Day 3 in our 4-day event is all about transportation. Packed with more guest speakers and topic-related games, we welcome you to a fun-filled day!

More

Sustainable Agriculture

Last but not least is day 4, centered around sustainable agriculture. We invite you to the last day of our event for this year and hope you leave with an educational and enlightening experience and everything you need to know to live a better life!

More

Interact With Others

Climate change is a global issue that affects us all, so it’s imperative that we discuss this issue with each other. Before and during the event, we provide an open forum where everyone can discuss facts about climate change and share the actions they take to combat it.
